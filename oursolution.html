What is i-Ruma?

i-Ruma combines various functions fundamental to property management such as:

Visitor Management

Circular and Announcement

Task Management

Facilities Management

Accounting

Collection Management

Reports

Type of Users

Residents

Easy way to check, pay bills, submit feedbacks and communicate with the committee. Bringing a more convenient environment

Guards

Able to get alerts for emergencies and for visitor tracking. Increased safety and peace of mind with an improved security experience

Property Manager

Efficiently handle day to day tasks while minimizing time wastage on admin work. Priority will be given to major issues

Technician / Handyman

Easy way to receive notification from owners on defects or damages in common area. No need to wait for management office staff to delegate to technician / handyman
